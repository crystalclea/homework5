New HIV infections in Hong Kong show slight decrease in 2016

The year 2016 marks a drop in the number of AIDs cases reported as compared to the previous year - a relief to aids concern groups.

Over the years, the number of HIV cases continued to rise. A total of 725 new cases of HIV infections were reported back in 2015, a 11.4 per cent increase from the 651 in 2014.
